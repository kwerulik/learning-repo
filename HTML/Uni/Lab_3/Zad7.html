A Chrismas Carol

By Charles Dickens

I have endeavoured in this Ghostly little book, to raise the Ghost of an Idea, which shall not put my readers out of humoer with themselfs, with each other, wtih the season, or with me. May it hunt their houses pleasantly, and no one with to lay it.

Their faithful Friend and Servant.
C. D. December. 1843

Stave 1: Marley's Ghost

Marley was dead: to begin with. There is no doubt whatever about that. The register of his burial was signed by the clergyman, the undertaker, and the chief mourner. Scroog signed it. And Scrooge's name was good upon 'Change, for anything he chose to put his hand to.
